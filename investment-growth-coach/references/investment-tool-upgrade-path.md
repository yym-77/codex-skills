# Investment Tool Upgrade Path

Tools follow ability, not impulse.

## Current Default

Start with off-exchange funds unless the user has passed higher-level gates.

## Fund To ETF Gate

Before live ETF practice, require:

- 4 weeks of decision review.
- Understanding of ETF trading, liquidity, bid-ask spread, premium/discount, tracking error, and costs.
- Ability to write buy reason, position cap, and invalidation condition.
- No repeated panic or FOMO decisions in recent reviews.

If not passed, use ETF simulation.

## ETF To Stock Gate

Before stock practice, require:

- Ability to read basic financial statements.
- Ability to explain business model, industry position, valuation, catalyst, and risk.
- Single-stock position cap.
- Clear invalidation and exit condition.
- Simulation review before live practice.

## Convertible Bond Gate

Default to learning and simulation until the user can explain conversion value, premium, bond floor, yield to maturity, forced redemption, downward revision, credit risk, and liquidity.

## Rule Freshness

Before real ETF, stock, or convertible-bond operations, verify current exchange rules, product documents, broker fee rules, and risk warnings. Do not treat any static checklist as permanent regulation.
