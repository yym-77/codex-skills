# Rebalancing Rules

Cash is part of the strategy.

## Cash Layers

- Basic reserve cash: emergency/life money. Do not invest it.
- Opportunity cash: used for controlled staged adding after confirmation.
- Extreme-drawdown cash: used only after deep drawdown plus risk-controlled evidence.

Do not spend all cash because of one down day.

## Rebalancing

Check monthly or after sharp moves:

- Has one high-volatility theme exceeded the user's cap?
- Are multiple funds actually holding the same theme?
- Did a strong theme become crowded?
- Has the original reason changed?
- Does the user still have enough cash to act later?

If overweight, prefer pause new buying, reduce base plan, or rebalance gradually. Do not force selling solely because something rose.
